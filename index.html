EMAC Inua Jamii program,

Meet your finance goals through a simplified loan.

EMAC is a non-governmental organization (NGO) working in partnership with the World Bank to empower small businesses and promote economic growth. Through its Inua Jamii program, EMAC provides accessible capital to entrepreneurs, enabling them to start or expand their businesses. The program offers flexible repayment plans ranging from six months to one year, with a low interest rate of 1%, making it affordable and sustainable for small-scale business owners. EMAC is committed to uplifting communities by supporting financial inclusion and fostering long-term business success.
